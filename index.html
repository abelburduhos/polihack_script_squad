The pipeline

Three stages, one map.

01

Detect

A mesh of forest sensors continuously scans for ignition signatures. Flood gauges along the Danube, Someș, and Bega watch water levels.

02

Verify

Each report is investigated by the nearest available drone. Range and battery are taken into account when picking the responder.

03

Respond

Confirmed events trigger emergency services. False alarms close silently — without ever waking a dispatcher.
